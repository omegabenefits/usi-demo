Benefit Resource Center (BRC)

We are here to advocate for the health needs of you and your family members that are enrolled in the benefit plans. This comes at no cost to you!

We can assist with the following:

  • Billing or provider issues
  • Claims questions
  • Appeals
  • Coordination of care
  • Second opinions
  • Medical care research
  • Much more!
