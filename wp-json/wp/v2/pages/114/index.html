          Qualified Life Event <\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
          \n\t\t\t\t
          \n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

          Certain life events provide you a 31-day window to elect benefits or change your covered dependents.<\/p>

          What is a Qualified Life Event?<\/strong> If you experience any of the scenarios below, you may be eligible to update your benefit elections mid-year:<\/p>

          • Marriage, divorce, or legal separation<\/li>
          • Birth or adoption of a child<\/li>
          • Commencement or termination of adoption proceedings<\/li>
          • Change in child’s dependent status<\/li>
          • Death of a dependent<\/li>
          • Loss or gain of other health coverage for you and\/or dependents<\/li>
          • Change in employment status<\/li>
          • Change in residence due to an employment transfer for you or your spouse<\/li>
          • Change in Medicaid\/Medicare eligibility for you or a dependent<\/li>
          • Receipt of a Qualified Medical Child Support Order<\/li><\/ul>

            Remember, dependent verification will be required.<\/p>
