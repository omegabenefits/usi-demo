Paid Time Off (PTO) <\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

How much time off you have available depends on your employment status:<\/p>

  • Exempt<\/li>
  • Non-Exempt<\/li><\/ul>

    Exempt employees<\/strong><\/p>

    There\u2019s no specified amount of vacation days or paid time off for full-time exempt employees, so we encourage you to work directly with your manager to arrange the time off you need when you need. We believe that taking time off is essential to the health and productivity of every employee.<\/p>

    All regular exempt employees, regardless of how many hours they work, accrue sick time and can use it for a variety of purposes related to their own care and the care of family as described in the Exempt Time Off policy.<\/p>

    Submit your Time Off via Workday > Time off & Leave > Request Time Off<\/strong><\/p>

    Non-exempt employees<\/strong><\/p>

    All non-exempt employees accrue two days of Personal Time Off (PTO) each month for their first five years of service (PTO is prorated for part-time employees). After five years, you\u2019ll begin to accrue more. You can use this PTO for vacation, sick time, personal business, or floating holidays. You are also required to use your PTO during the July and December company break periods.<\/p>
