New This Year<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

We engaged Synergy Healthcare to manage the Employee Wellness program replacing Discover Health. In 2022, employees will benefit by paying lower medical plan payroll deductions when they meet Synergy\u2019s wellness requirements.<\/p>

By April 1, 2022, employees must complete a Synergy Health Assessment and send Synergy their biometric screening results.\u00a0 They must also maintain contact with a Synergy Advocate as requested throughout the year.<\/p>

Employees must have a preventive Primary Care Visit by November 30, 2022 in order to keep the lowest per paycheck cost for coverage in 2023.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
